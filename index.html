AI Lab — Self-Learning Engine
The AI picks strategies, back-tests its own picks on historical data, logs each win and loss, and adjusts how much it trusts each strategy. That learned confidence then shapes the strategy pick, entry, stop, and targets you see on the dashboard. Train it more to sharpen its edge.
Train the AI
Each test reads a random historical chart, picks the fitting strategy, then walks the trade forward bar-by-bar on real data to see whether the target or the stop hit first — and updates that strategy's confidence. Runs on real market data, so it needs a connection and takes a few seconds per test.
Live learning console
Every self-test streams here: the setup it picked, the planned entry / stop / target, whether the target or stop hit first on real forward data, the result in R (multiples of risk), and how that nudged the strategy's confidence — which feeds straight into your dashboard reads and trade levels.
