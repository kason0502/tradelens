## 2026-06-26 (session 12) ‚Äî fix "nothing loads" (proxy flooding + slow sequential fetch)
- **Root cause:** the new background pollers (movers/watchlist/indices on timers) were hammering the shared CORS proxies into rate-limiting, AND every lookup tried proxies **sequentially** (2 passes √ó 2 hosts √ó 5 proxies ‚âà up to ~140s) so a busy feed hung instead of failing.
- **Parallel proxy race** ‚Äî new `raceAttempts(url)` fires all proxies at once and resolves on the first valid Yahoo chart; `tryFetch`, `fetchQuote` intraday, and `fetchLivePrice` all use it. Result: lookups now resolve in **~150ms** when proxies are up, and fail fast (~7s) when they're not. Trimmed 2 dead proxies, added 4 more providers, timeout 9s‚Üí7s.
- **Background pollers stand down** while you're analyzing a ticker (`window.__userFetch` gate + `bgIdle()` checks page-visible / not-landing), start **staggered**, and refresh far less often (indices 90s‚Üí5m, watchlist 60s‚Üí4m, movers 2m‚Üí6m) so they never starve your lookups. `liveTick` also yields during a lookup.
